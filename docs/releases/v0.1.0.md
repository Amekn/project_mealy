## What changed

- This was Mealy's first immutable production release.
- Linux x86-64 and ARM64 received attested rootless archives and Debian
  packages with durable execution, recovery, replay, governed tools, and
  owner-operated services.
- macOS received a conversation-only preview; governed worker profiles were
  intentionally unavailable there.
- Public release acceptance reinstalled the published artifacts and checked
  their lifecycle independently of the build jobs.
